# Hypothesis Framework

## Key Hypothesis
Digital transformation initiatives can significantly improve operational efficiency by reducing cycle time, lowering costs, and enabling faster, data-driven decisions.

## Supporting Hypotheses
H1: Process inefficiencies and manual interventions increase cycle time and operational costs.

H2: Automation of repetitive tasks reduces processing time and cost per transaction.

H3: Analytics dashboards improve management visibility and decision-making speed.

H4: Digital interventions improve overall workforce productivity.
